Hostel Management System – Requirement Notes

We are planning to build a hostel management system for students and administrators.

1. The system shall allow students to register using their email ID.
2. The system must store student personal details securely.
3. Admin should be able to approve or reject applications.
4. The application should be fast and user-friendly.
5. The system shall generate monthly occupancy reports.
6. It should support around 500 concurrent users.
7. The system must provide secure login functionality.
8. Notifications will be sent to students after room allocation.
9. The software should be flexible for future expansion.
10. The system shall maintain records of payments.
11. The application should work efficiently even during peak hours.
12. The system must allow admin to search student records.
13. Backup should be taken regularly.
14. The interface should be simple.
15. The system shall respond within 2 seconds for most operations.

Additional Notes:
The project deadline is December.
The development team will use modern tools.
